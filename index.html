STProof 全场景事件确权协议

为一切客观事实提供可验证、不可篡改、永久存证的链上确权服务。

STProof Universal Event Verification Protocol

Providing verifiable, immutable, permanent on-chain verification for all objective facts.

全场景事件类型
Universal Event Types

有实物事件

示例：实寄封邮寄、艺术品创作、文物出土、瓷器烧制

实物仅作为事件发生的现场载体，协议对「实物相关的客观事件」确权，而非实物本身。实物可保留或销毁，与链上NFT无权属绑定。

Physical Object Events

First day covers, artwork creation, cultural relics, manufacturing

The physical object is only evidence of the event. STProof verifies the objective event, not ownership of the object. The physical item may be kept or destroyed without affecting the NFT.

无实物事件

示例：线上发布、观点声明、著作权、学术发表

纯数字/行为类事件，无实物载体。依托时间戳、数字签名、第三方存档形成不可篡改存证。

Digital-Only Events

Online publications, statements, copyright, academic releases

Pure digital or behavioral events without physical carriers. Secured by timestamp, digital signature, and third-party archiving.

混合事件

示例：新闻现场、会议活动、跨境存证、法律取证

融合物理现场与数字记录，全维度采集证据，适用于复杂场景的确权。

Hybrid Events

Live news, conferences, cross-border proof, legal evidence

Combines physical and digital records for comprehensive, multi-dimensional verification in complex scenarios.
