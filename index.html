协议核心理念

以平权为核心，重新定义链上确权，让事实成为数字世界的可信基础设施

确权对象：唯事件

统一以「客观发生的历史事件」为确权核心，非实体资产或数字内容。实物仅为事件现场载体，无实物事件直接存证，彻底摒弃RWA，从底层规避监管风险。

核心理念：平权存证

事实记录无资金门槛，任何人只需支付Gas费即可铸造Event NFT，无需额外费用。技术不制造新的不平等，人人都有权为真实事件建立永久链上凭证。

价值机制：引用税

事件价值不依赖少数人炒作，而是由被相信、被使用的次数决定。每一次链上引用都是对真实的投票，引用费为发起人带来持续收益，让价值回归影响力。

生态定位：重型事实层

不与轻量级协议竞争，专注「高价值重型事实」锚定，需多源证据、物理验证、终极仲裁的事件皆可适配，与轻量级协议互补，构建多层级数字可信体系。

全场景事件类型

覆盖有实物、无实物、混合三类事件，协议具备无限扩展性，社区可自定义事件模板

有实物事件

示例：实寄封邮寄、艺术品创作、文物出土、瓷器烧制

实物仅作为事件发生的现场载体，协议对「实物相关的客观事件」确权，而非实物本身。实物可选择保留或销毁，与链上NFT无权属、价值绑定关系。

无实物事件

示例：线上发布、链上投票、观点声明、学术发表

纯数字/行为类事件，无伴随实物，协议直接对事件本身进行多源证据采集、存证与确权。依托链上时间戳、数字签名、第三方存档完成证据闭环。

混合事件

示例：新闻现场、演出活动、法律存证、线下会议

有实物但非核心，或实物与数字并存的事件。融合有实物/无实物事件的证据验证规则，兼顾物理现场与数字记录，实现全维度事实存证。

Not Proof of Asset, But Proof of Event
What Happens, Exists; What's Proven, Trusted

STProof (Spatial-Temporal Proof) is a universal on-chain event proof and value capture protocol.
Breaking the traditional asset-mapping mindset, it transforms objective historical events into immutable permanent records in the digital world through multi-source evidence cross-verification, trusted timestamps, and decentralized storage.
Proof for tangible and intangible events alike, zero financial barrier for fact recording, value derived from trust and usage.

Core Concepts

Equity-centered, redefining on-chain proof, making facts the trusted infrastructure of the digital world

Proof Object: Event Only

Unifiedly takes "objective historical events" as the core of proof, not physical assets or digital content. Physical items are only event carriers; intangible events are directly proven, completely abandoning RWA to avoid regulatory risks at the bottom layer.

Core Philosophy: Equitable Proof

Zero financial barrier for fact recording. Anyone can mint Event NFTs by only paying Gas fees, no extra costs. Technology creates no new inequality; everyone has the right to establish permanent on-chain credentials for real events.

Value Mechanism: Citation Royalty

Event value does not depend on hype by a few, but on the number of times it is trusted and used. Every on-chain citation is a vote for truth, and citation fees bring continuous revenue to the initiator, returning value to influence.

Ecosystem Position: Heavy Fact Layer

Not competing with lightweight protocols, focusing on anchoring "high-value heavy facts". Events requiring multi-source evidence, physical verification, and ultimate arbitration are all adaptable, complementing lightweight protocols to build a multi-level digital trust system.

Full-Scenario Event Types

Covering tangible, intangible, and hybrid events, the protocol has unlimited scalability, and the community can customize event templates

Tangible Events

Example: First Day Cover Mailing, Art Creation, Cultural Relic Excavation, Porcelain Firing

Physical items are only carriers of the event. The protocol proves "objective events related to physical items", not the items themselves. Items can be retained or destroyed, with no ownership or value binding to on-chain NFTs.

Intangible Events

Example: Online Release, On-Chain Voting, Opinion Statement, Academic Publication

Pure digital/behavioral events with no accompanying physical items. The protocol directly collects, proves, and certifies the event itself, completing the evidence closed-loop through on-chain timestamps, digital signatures, and third-party archiving.

Hybrid Events

Example: News Scene, Live Performance, Legal Proof, Offline Conference

Events with physical items but not core, or coexisting physical and digital elements. Integrating evidence verification rules for tangible/intangible events, balancing physical scenes and digital records to achieve full-dimensional fact proof.
