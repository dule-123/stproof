混合事件

有实物但非核心，或实物与数字并存的事件，融合物理现场与数字记录实现全维度事实存证

混合事件核心特征

实物+数字双维度

事件既包含物理现场的实物元素，也包含数字记录/数字行为，二者共同构成完整事件事实。

多模态证据验证

融合有实物事件的物理证据（现场影像、GPS、实物凭证）和无实物事件的数字证据（时间戳、签名），验证维度更全面。

实物非核心载体

实物仅作为事件发生的场景佐证，而非确权核心，即使实物灭失也不影响事件本身的链上存证有效性。

场景适配性强

覆盖绝大多数现实场景的事件类型，是STProof协议应用最广泛的事件类别。

典型应用场景

新闻现场

记者在新闻现场的采访行为存证，包含现场影像（物理）+ 发布记录（数字），证明采访行为的真实性与时间线。

演出活动

线下演出的举办事实存证，包含现场门票/物料（物理）+ 直播记录/购票数据（数字），固定演出举办的完整证据链。

法律存证

线下签约、取证等法律行为存证，包含纸质文件（物理）+ 电子签名/时间戳（数字），满足法律证据的完整性要求。

线下会议

线下会议的举办与决议存证，包含会议现场（物理）+ 签到记录/决议文件（数字），证明会议过程与结果的真实性。

Hybrid Events

Events with physical items but not core, or coexisting physical and digital elements, integrating physical scenes and digital records to achieve full-dimensional fact proof

Core Characteristics of Hybrid Events

Dual Dimensions: Physical + Digital

Events include both physical elements of the on-site scene and digital records/digital behaviors, which together form the complete event facts.

Multi-modal Evidence Verification

Integrate physical evidence (on-site images, GPS, physical vouchers) of tangible events and digital evidence (timestamps, signatures) of intangible events for more comprehensive verification.

Physical Items as Non-core Carriers

Physical items only serve as scene evidence for the occurrence of events, not the core of proof. Even if physical items are lost, it does not affect the validity of on-chain proof of the event itself.

Strong Scene Adaptability

Covers most event types in real-world scenarios, and is the most widely used event category of the STProof protocol.

Typical Application Scenarios

News Scene

Proof of journalists' interview behavior at news scenes, including on-site images (physical) + release records (digital), to prove the authenticity and timeline of interview behavior.

Performance Events

Proof of the holding fact of offline performances, including on-site tickets/materials (physical) + live broadcast records/ticketing data (digital), to fix the complete evidence chain of performance holding.

Legal Proof

Proof of legal behaviors such as offline signing and evidence collection, including paper documents (physical) + electronic signatures/timestamps (digital), meeting the integrity requirements of legal evidence.

Offline Meetings

Proof of the holding and resolutions of offline meetings, including meeting scenes (physical) + check-in records/resolution documents (digital), to prove the authenticity of meeting processes and results.
