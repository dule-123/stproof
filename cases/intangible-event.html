无实物事件

纯数字/行为类事件，无伴随实物，协议直接对事件本身进行多源证据采集、存证与确权

无实物事件核心特征

无物理载体

事件发生全程无实物参与，仅以数字形式、行为形式存在，如线上发言、链上操作、观点发布等。

多源数字证据

依托链上时间戳、数字签名、第三方平台存档、IPFS去中心化存储完成证据闭环，确保事件真实性。

低成本存证

无需物理证据采集、运输、存储成本，仅需完成数字证据的链上固化，存证门槛更低。

即时确权

事件发生后可立即完成多源证据采集与链上存证，无需等待实物验证周期，确权效率更高。

典型应用场景

线上发布

文章、视频、代码、创意方案等数字内容的首次发布时间与内容确权，防止抄袭与篡改。

链上投票

DAO提案投票、社区决策等链上行为的过程与结果存证，确保投票行为不可篡改、可追溯。

观点声明

个人/机构对特定事件的观点、立场声明存证，固定声明时间与内容，避免事后抵赖。

学术发表

学术论文、研究成果的预发布存证，证明创作时间线，解决学术成果归属争议。

Intangible Events

Pure digital/behavioral events with no accompanying physical items, the protocol directly collects, proves and certifies the event itself with multi-source evidence

Core Characteristics of Intangible Events

No Physical Carrier

Events occur entirely without physical participation, existing only in digital or behavioral forms, such as online speeches, on-chain operations, opinion releases, etc.

Multi-source Digital Evidence

Complete evidence closed-loop through on-chain timestamps, digital signatures, third-party platform archiving, and IPFS decentralized storage to ensure event authenticity.

Low-cost Proof

No costs for physical evidence collection, transportation, and storage, only need to complete on-chain solidification of digital evidence, with lower proof thresholds.

Instant Certification

Multi-source evidence collection and on-chain proof can be completed immediately after the event occurs, no need to wait for physical verification cycles, with higher certification efficiency.

Typical Application Scenarios

Online Release

Certification of the first release time and content of digital content such as articles, videos, code, and creative plans to prevent plagiarism and tampering.

On-Chain Voting

Proof of the process and results of on-chain behaviors such as DAO proposal voting and community decision-making to ensure voting behaviors are immutable and traceable.

Opinion Statement

Proof of personal/institutional opinions and position statements on specific events, fixing the statement time and content to avoid repudiation afterwards.

Academic Publication

Pre-release proof of academic papers and research results to prove the creation timeline and resolve disputes over the ownership of academic achievements.
